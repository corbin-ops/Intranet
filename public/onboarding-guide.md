## 🌴 Time Off & Work-Life Balance Policy

At DewClaw Land, we believe that a well-rested team is a productive team. We're committed to supporting your work-life balance while ensuring our clients and team always have the coverage they need to succeed.

### Our Philosophy

We trust you to manage your time responsibly. Here's how we make it work:

**Work is completed first.** Your projects and responsibilities are your priority. Time off is approved when your work is on track and deadlines are met.

**Coverage is maintained.** We ensure that your absence doesn't leave your team hanging. Coordination with your manager and team ensures smooth operations.

**Balance is valued.** We genuinely want you to recharge and spend time with family, friends, and yourself. A balanced team member is a happy and productive team member.

### Paid Time Off (PTO) Breakdown

#### Eligibility & Accrual

| Milestone | Paid Days | Details |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **First 3 Months** | 0 days | Probationary period—focus on onboarding and learning |
| **After 3 Months** | 10 days/year | Accrued monthly (0.83 days per month) |
| **Additional Days** | Unpaid | Any time off beyond your 10 paid days is unpaid |

#### How Accrual Works

Once you complete your first 3 months, you begin accruing paid time off at a rate of approximately 0.83 days per month. Here's an example:

- **Month 4:** 0.83 days accrued
- **Month 5:** 1.66 days accrued
- **Month 6:** 2.49 days accrued
- **Month 7:** 3.32 days accrued
- **Month 8:** 4.15 days accrued
- **Month 9:** 4.98 days accrued
- **Month 10:** 5.81 days accrued
- **Month 11:** 6.64 days accrued
- **Month 12:** 7.47 days accrued
- **Month 13:** 8.30 days accrued
- **Month 14:** 9.13 days accrued
- **Month 15:** 10 days accrued (full year reached!)

**Note:** Your accrual resets annually on your hire date anniversary.

### Time Off Request Process

#### The 14-Day Advance Notice Rule

To request time off, please follow this process:

1. **Submit Request 14 Days in Advance:** Provide your manager with at least 14 days' notice before your planned time off. This gives us time to plan coverage and ensure your work is properly delegated.

2. **Discuss Coverage:** Work with your manager to ensure your responsibilities are covered during your absence. This might mean:
   - Delegating tasks to team members
   - Scheduling your time off around key projects
   - Setting up auto-responders and communication plans

3. **Get Approval:** Your manager will confirm approval once they've verified that your work is on track and coverage is arranged.

4. **Update the Calendar:** Once approved, mark your time off on the shared calendar so the whole team knows you'll be away.

#### Emergency Time Off

Life happens! If you need emergency time off with less than 14 days' notice:

- **Contact your manager immediately** to discuss the situation
- **Explain the circumstances** and how long you'll need
- **Work together on coverage** to minimize disruption
- **Emergency time off may be unpaid** depending on the situation and your available accrued time

We understand that emergencies occur, and we'll work with you to find a solution.

### Types of Time Off

#### Paid Time Off (PTO)

Use your 10 annual paid days for:
- Vacation and personal travel
- Personal appointments and errands
- Mental health days and rest
- Anything you need time for!

#### Sick Leave

If you're ill or unable to work:
- **Notify your manager as soon as possible**
- **Provide updates** on when you expect to return
- **Sick leave is separate** from your PTO allocation and doesn't count against your 10 days
- **Documentation may be required** for absences longer than 2 consecutive days

#### Holidays

DewClaw Land observes holidays in multiple regions. Check the **Holidays tab** in the intranet for the complete list of observed holidays in your region (USA, Philippines, Egypt, South Africa).

#### Unpaid Time Off

If you need more time off than your 10 paid days:

- **Discuss with your manager** well in advance (ideally 30+ days notice)
- **Ensure your work is covered** and deadlines are met
- **Get written approval** from your manager and HR
- **This time will be unpaid** and may affect your benefits depending on duration

### Work-Life Balance Expectations

We support your balance by:

- **Respecting boundaries:** Don't work outside your scheduled hours unless it's an emergency
- **Encouraging time off:** Use your PTO! A rested team is a productive team
- **Flexible arrangements:** Talk to your manager about flexible schedules if you need them
- **No "always on" culture:** We value presence during work hours, not constant availability
- **Supporting wellness:** Take care of yourself mentally and physically

### Important Reminders

**For Managers:**
- Approve time off requests when work is on track and coverage is arranged
- Encourage your team to use their PTO—it's healthy!
- Plan ahead to ensure coverage during team absences
- Be flexible with emergency situations

**For Team Members:**
- Give 14 days' notice whenever possible
- Work with your manager on coverage planning
- Complete your work before taking time off
- Use your PTO—you've earned it!
- Communicate clearly about your availability

### Questions About Time Off?

Reach out to HR or your manager:
- 💬 **Manager:** Day-to-day time off requests and coverage
- 💼 **HR Team:** Policy questions, accrual calculations, special circumstances
- 📧 **Email:** hello@dewclawland.com for formal requests or documentation
