# Company Intranet Design Brainstorm

## Design Approach: Modern Professional with Warm Elegance

**Design Movement**: Contemporary Corporate with Human-Centered Design

**Core Principles**:
1. **Clarity First**: Information hierarchy that guides employees naturally through sections without cognitive overload
2. **Warmth & Accessibility**: Soften corporate formality with approachable typography and breathing space
3. **Scannable Layouts**: Visual separation of concerns—org chart, SOPs, holidays, reports each have distinct visual zones
4. **Intentional Depth**: Subtle shadows and layering create visual hierarchy without flatness

**Color Philosophy**:
- Primary: Deep slate blue (#1e3a5f) for trust and professionalism
- Accent: Warm coral (#f97316) for CTAs and highlights—humanizes the interface
- Neutrals: Off-white backgrounds (#fafbfc) with charcoal text (#1a1a1a)
- Reasoning: Blue conveys corporate stability; coral adds warmth and energy, breaking monotony

**Layout Paradigm**:
- Asymmetric dashboard with sidebar navigation
- Hero section featuring org chart with visual hierarchy
- Card-based sections for SOPs, reports, and holidays
- Generous whitespace between sections to reduce cognitive load

**Signature Elements**:
1. **Circular Avatar Frames**: Team member photos in soft-bordered circles with subtle hover elevation
2. **Organizational Tree Visualization**: Hierarchical display with connecting lines showing reporting relationships
3. **Color-Coded Holiday Calendar**: Region-specific colors (Philippines=gold, Egypt=teal, US=red, Africa=green)

**Interaction Philosophy**:
- Smooth transitions when hovering over team members (slight scale, shadow enhancement)
- Expandable sections for SOPs and reports (accordion-style)
- Calendar hover states show holiday details
- Click-through to full documents with consistent back navigation

**Animation**:
- Fade-in animations on page load (200ms, ease-out)
- Subtle hover scale (1.02x) on interactive cards
- Smooth expand/collapse for accordion sections (300ms)
- No excessive motion—professionalism over flashiness

**Typography System**:
- Display: Poppins Bold (headings) - modern, friendly, professional
- Body: Inter Regular (content) - clean, readable, corporate
- Hierarchy: H1 (32px), H2 (24px), H3 (18px), Body (14px)
- Line height: 1.6 for body text, 1.3 for headings

---

## Selected Approach: Modern Professional with Warm Elegance

This design balances corporate professionalism with human warmth, making the intranet feel like a welcoming resource rather than a sterile information dump. The color palette combines trustworthy blue with approachable coral, while asymmetric layouts and generous spacing prevent information fatigue.
